You need to empathise with your users

22/Jul 2015 9 min. read

User Experience User empathy

Won’t empathise with your users? You won’t solve their problems. Software applications are created for people to use. This is a pretty obvious statement for anyone who has ever operated a modern digital device, moreso anyone involved in software creation. However, historically, it was an alien concept that users should be at the centre of the software design process. Only recently do we see a trend towards trying to understand more about the user, their motivations, goals and required outcomes.
