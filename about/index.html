A bit about me

I’ve been working as an interactive designer and developer since about 2001. I’ve been a designer, a developer (LAMP and Javascript mostly) and that has all lead me to where I am now, a user experience engineer.

Not Another UX Blog!

Well, yes. But I’m hoping that this one will be different (of course I’d say that). My plan here is to only talk about stuff that either:

  • I’ve done myself and found useful
  • I’ve seen demonstrated and found useful

I’m writing this as much as a note pad for my future self as for the world. Making notes, and having a reference for things I’ve picked up really helps me get things done faster, as I’m no longer thinking “How did I do that last time?”, but “What can I do next to improve?”.
